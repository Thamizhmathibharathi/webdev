Placements

The department of Computer Science and Engineering was established during the year 1996. The undergraduate program B.E (CSE) has been accredited by NBA. The department has state-of-the-art laboratory facilities, Wi-Fi enabled classrooms with laptops and overhead projectors to facilitate e-Learning, research laboratories with high-speed computing machines, a well-equipped seminar hall, a fully equipped conference hall and a well-stocked department library for the holistic development of the students.

The students are provided with the following opportunities:

  • Higher Studies in Indian and abroad Universities
  • Placements in Super Dream Companies with salary packages more than 1 Crore.
  • Entrepreneurship cell
  • Incubation and Innovation opportunities
  • Internal Funding Projects opportunities
  • Internship in Indian and abroad universities/companies
  • Research publications in reputed journals
  • Participation in technical competitions and Hackathons
  • Involvement in Professional Societies such as IEEE, CSI, ACM, ACM-W
  • Consultancy project opportunities
  • Access to online courses through platforms like Coursera, NPTEL, Swayam etc.
  • Guest lectures from Industries
  • Outreach programs
  • Curriculum
  • Lab Infrastructure
  • Student Co-curricular Activities and Achievements
  • Program Assessment Committee
