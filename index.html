Vision

To emerge as a world class technology department through education, innovation and collaborative research.

Mission

  • To impart quality education to students.
  • To create and disseminate knowledge for the betterment of mankind.
  • To establish a center of excellence in collaboration with industries, research laboratories and other agencies to meet the changing needs of society.
  • To provide individual attention and enable character building.
  • To encourage entrepreneurship skills among students.

Department Highlights

  • Dedicated and highly qualified faculty members who inculcate both theoretical knowledge and practical skills to the students in a balanced manner.
  • Moodle Learning Management System (LMS) which hosts individual course pages comprising the syllabus, lesson plan, lecture notes and reading materials. The pedagogy used is primarily learner centric.
  • Periodical guest lectures by illustrious academics from well-known universities or seasoned experts from several prestigious companies.
  • Several workshops on emerging technologies for the benefit of students to supplement their curriculum. This facilitates our students to enrich their knowledge and also be up to date on current industry trends.
  • Active student chapters of professional societies such as ACM, CSI and IEEE as well as a CodeChef campus chapter which regularly conduct coding competitions and sessions on open-source software development.
  • Short Term Training Programs (STTP), Faculty Development Programs (FDP) and National Seminars sponsored by AICTE, Anna University and ACM Education Committee.
  • Yearly, international conferences and national conferences to get deeper insights in the recent areas of research.
  • Almost all faculty members are recognized Ph.D. supervisors for pursuing research in Anna University, Chennai.
  • At present, twenty two part time research scholars and ten full time research scholars are pursuing their Ph.Ds.
  • Ten patents have been filed by the faculty members of the Department, out of which four have been published in the Indian Patent Journal.
  • External funded projects from various funding agencies such as DST, AICTE, Tamil Virtual academy, CSI, Chellammal Agro Agency, Nvidia, BIRAC and ISRO have been completed.
  • MoUs with Caterpillar Ltd., Manatec Electronics Private Limited, Pondicherry, Vimana Technologies Pvt. Ltd (Group Company of Aspec Scire Inc), Bangalore, Intellect Design Arena, Sigaram Technologies, ACI Automation facilitates the students to pursue summer/winter internships and faculty to engage in collaborative consultancy research projects.
