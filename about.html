About the Department

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) was established in the year 1996 since the inception of the college. The primary objective of CSE department is to provide a high quality computer science education to students from all over India as well as abroad and across all economic strata. The department offers an undergraduate degree program B.E(CSE) and a postgraduate degree program M.E(CSE). In 2006, the department was recognized as a Centre for Research, by Anna University to offer a Ph.D. program. The undergraduate program B.E (CSE) has been reaccredited by NBA in 2021 and the postgraduate program M.E (CSE) has been accredited by NBA in 2016. The department has state-of-the-art laboratory facilities, Wi-Fi enabled class rooms with laptops to facilitate e-Learning, a well-equipped seminar hall and a well-stocked department library.

Programs Offered

  • B.E. Computer Science and Engineering
  • M.E. Computer Science and Engineering
  • Integrated M.Tech CSE

Department Objectives

  • Provide quality education in core and emerging areas of computing.
  • Promote interdisciplinary research and innovation.
  • Develop industry-ready professionals with strong ethical values.

Vision

To be recognized as a center of excellence in Computer Science and Engineering, nurturing professionals and innovators to solve real-world challenges.

Industry Collaborations

The department actively collaborates with leading IT companies and research organizations to enhance academic and professional development.
