About Our Events

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering organizes a wide range of academic and co-curricular events every year. These events are designed to enhance technical knowledge, encourage innovation, foster leadership, and provide students with real-world exposure. Workshops, hackathons, symposiums, guest lectures, and cultural events are integral parts of our department's calendar.

Events (Academic Year 2024–2025)

National Level Hackathon

A 24-hour coding competition where students build innovative solutions for real-world problems.

AI & Machine Learning Workshop

A hands-on workshop introducing students to the latest tools in AI and ML development.

Guest Lecture Series

Industry experts from Google, Microsoft, and TCS share their experiences and insights.

Student Project Expo

Final year students showcase their innovative projects and research to peers and industry representatives.

Annual Technical Symposium

An inter-college technical fest with coding contests, paper presentations, and tech quizzes.

Cultural Fest – TechnoVibe

A blend of technology and culture, showcasing talents in both technical and cultural domains.

Placement Training Program

Intensive sessions on aptitude, coding, and soft skills to prepare students for campus recruitment.

CodeSprint Challenge

A monthly competitive programming contest conducted by the CSE Coding Club.
