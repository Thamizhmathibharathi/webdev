About CSE Clubs

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering encourages students to actively participate in various technical and non-technical clubs. These clubs provide opportunities to explore leadership, teamwork, innovation, and research skills while keeping students updated with the latest trends in computer science and technology.

Our Clubs

CodeCraft Club

The CodeCraft Club focuses on competitive programming, problem solving, and coding challenges. It regularly conducts coding contests and training sessions to sharpen logical thinking.

AI & ML Club

A club dedicated to Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning where students explore real-world applications, build projects, and conduct workshops on deep learning and data science.

Cyber Security Club

This club raises awareness about cyber threats, ethical hacking, and digital forensics. Students work on projects related to online safety and cybercrime investigation.

Robotics & IoT Club

The Robotics & IoT Club provides hands-on experience in building robots, automation systems, and Internet of Things applications. Students design innovative hardware-software solutions.

Web Development Club

This club encourages students to design creative websites and applications. Members learn about frontend, backend, and full-stack development with modern frameworks.
